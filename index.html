Goal

Produce a synthesized document and guidelines to help address the impact of drought on the livelihoods and ecosystems in Ethiopia using a citizen science based consultative approach.

 

Purpose

The purpose of this form is to solicit all sorts of ideas (big and small) and resources based on the three dimensional solution space: insight, technology and coordination. We reach out to Ethiopians and friends of Ethiopia; from all sectors and backgrounds: farmers, extensions agents, educators, scientists, engineers and policy makers etc. The entries will be synthesized using a network of volunteers
